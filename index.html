Instructions:

English to Morse Code:

Enter your message into the first text field. Do not include punctuation. Press the encode button, and your translated message will appear in the second text field.

Morse Code to English:

Enter your message into the second text field using dit "." and dah "-". Morse Code letters must be separated by a space " ", and words must be separated by "//". Press the decode button, and your translated message will appear in the first text field. Click below to begin!
